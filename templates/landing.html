Crinkle

Crinkle is a URL shortener that allows you to shorten your links and track them.

I don't have enough time/effort to make a pretty landing page, so here's some features and screenshots instead.

Register




Simplified Dashboard

The dashboard makes it easy to quickly shorten a URL, with options to set a custom domain, alias, and password. You can also see all of your links at a glance.

Password Protect Links

Have sensitive links you want to shorten? Add a password intermediary screen to your link.

Custom Domains

Want to use your own domain? You can add it to your account and use it for your links.

Detailed Analytics

Want to see how your links are doing? You can see detailed analytics for each link, including the number of clicks, the referrers, cities, regions, countries, browsers, platforms, and devices.

QR Codes

Need a QR code to put on a flyer or to share with someone easily? Crinkle auto generates QR Codes.

Editing/Deletion

You can edit a QR code or delete it at any time.
